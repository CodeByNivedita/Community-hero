# Community Hero

## Tagline

**AI-Powered Hyperlocal Civic Problem Solver**

---

# Project Vision

Community Hero is an AI-powered platform that enables citizens to report, verify, track, and resolve civic infrastructure problems in their locality.

The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence, geolocation, community collaboration, and gamification to create a transparent, engaging, and efficient civic reporting ecosystem.

Instead of simply reporting issues, citizens become active contributors toward improving their community.

---

# Problem Statement

Communities frequently encounter civic issues such as:

- Potholes
- Water leakages
- Garbage accumulation
- Damaged streetlights
- Broken roads
- Drainage problems

Existing complaint systems are fragmented, slow, difficult to track, and often lack transparency.

Many reports remain unresolved because of duplicate reports, lack of verification, or poor citizen engagement.

Citizens rarely receive updates, making them lose trust in the reporting system.

---

# Solution

Community Hero provides an AI-powered civic reporting platform where citizens can:

- Upload photos or videos of civic issues.
- Automatically detect issue type using Gemini AI.
- Track issue progress in real time.
- Verify nearby issues.
- Earn rewards for helping the community.
- View community impact analytics.

Officials receive verified reports along with AI-generated insights, making issue resolution faster and more transparent.

# Key Features

## AI Issue Detection

Users upload an image.

Gemini AI automatically identifies

- Issue Category
- Severity
- Priority
- Suggested Department
- Confidence Score

---

## Interactive Community Map

Display all reported issues on an interactive map.

Features:

- Current Location
- Nearby Issues
- Filters
- Heatmap
- Navigation

---

## Community Verification

Nearby citizens can physically visit reported locations and verify issues.

Verification requires:

- GPS validation
- Live image upload
- AI comparison

Verified reports become more trustworthy.

---

## Gamified Rewards

Users earn XP and points for contributing.

Examples:

- Verified report
- Field verification
- Uploading additional evidence
- Daily missions

Users unlock

- Levels
- Badges
- Streaks
- Leaderboards

---

## Problem GO Inspired Missions

Users receive nearby verification missions.

Example:

"Walk 150 meters to verify a pothole."

Successful verification rewards XP and increases trust score.

---

## Impact Dashboard

Displays

- Community Health Score
- Resolution Rate
- Monthly Trends
- AI Insights
- Heatmaps
- Water Saved
- Garbage Removed
- Before & After Gallery

---

## AI Assistant

Citizens can ask:

- Where are nearby issues?
- Which area needs verification?
- Show unresolved reports.
- Explain why my report was rejected.
